Cut Flowers
We specialize in cultivating cut flowers, delivering them to florists in Srinagar and beyond, perfect for weddings and special events. We also provide ready-made bouquets and bunches, as well as flower plants, seeds, and bulbs.
Aromatics
From the soothing essence of lavender and rosemary to the timeless allure of rose and geranium. Immerse yourself in the artistry and craftsmanship of essential oils, dried flower buds, and the delicate beauty of aromatic hydrosols.
Nursery
From vibrant flowers and plants to fruit-bearing wonders like High-Density apples, apricots, peaches, plums, and kiwi. Elevate your garden experience with our quality plants, seeds, bulbs, and saplings, bringing nature’s abundance to your doorstep.
